Who We Are

NÓOS combines proven methods of modern self-leadership with centuries-old somatic practices and neuroscientific findings into holistic learning formats.

Our Philosophy

Soma & Ratio for Effective Leadership

Modern leadership demands far more than just intellectual concepts. In a complex, fast-paced world, emotional self-regulation, clarity, and a calm, present projection are key success factors. We call this symbiosis of mind and body Soma & Ratio.

NÓOS was conceived as a safe space where executives and ambitious professionals can step out of everyday pressure. Away from rigid conference rooms and sterile slide decks, we use the powerful backdrop of the Cyclades island of Amorgos to trigger sustainable behavioral changes.

Our methods are supported by current findings in neurobiology and psychology, complemented by traditional movement forms such as Kung-Fu and Yoga that make presence and relaxation physically experienceable. We empower you not only to evaluate stress cognitively, but to actively regulate it on a nervous and somatic level.

The Team

Expertise to Guide You

Constantin Gerlach

Founder & Lead Facilitator

As an experienced leadership coach and long-time practitioner of traditional Asian movement arts (Kung-Fu, Qi Gong), Constantin founded NÓOS. His passion lies in translating physical posture and presence work into measurable mental leadership qualities for the modern corporate routine.

Dr. Maria Kyriacou

Co-Facilitator & Neuroscientist

Maria holds a PhD in cognitive neuroscience and has researched the neurobiological mechanisms of stress and meditation for over 10 years. At NÓOS, she builds the scientific bridge, explaining how you can harness your brain's plasticity for effective stress resilience.
