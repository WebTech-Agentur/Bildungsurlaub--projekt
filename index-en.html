FACTS ON RECOGNITION

What Exactly is Educational Leave?

Educational leave (also known as Bildungsfreistellung) is a legally anchored right for employees to pursue further training during working hours while continuing to receive their salary.

In 14 out of 16 German federal states (except Bavaria and Saxony), almost all employees have a statutory entitlement to usually 5 paid days of training per calendar year (or 10 days combined over two years). Our program is officially recognized in the respective states as a health-promoting, personal development, and professional training measure.
