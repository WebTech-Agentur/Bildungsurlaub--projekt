Elements from martial arts are translated into a modern leadership context in this module. The focus lies on grounding, spatial awareness, and the conscious handling of pressure situations.

Building on this, various established breathing techniques are practiced, including calm, rhythmic, and activating breathing patterns. The goal is to use breath as a conscious tool for self-regulation, especially in high-pressure situations. This helps participants better manage stress responses and restore mental clarity more effectively in daily life.

FACTS ON RECOGNITION

What Exactly is Educational Leave?

Educational leave (also known as Bildungsfreistellung) is a legally anchored right for employees to pursue further training during working hours while continuing to receive their salary.

In most federal states (except Bavaria and Saxony), employees are typically entitled to up to five paid training days per year, sometimes accumulable over a two-year period. This program is officially recognized as a professional, health-promoting, and personal development-oriented educational course and complies with the respective legal requirements of the German federal states.

LEGAL BASIS

Legal Framework

Educational leave is legally regulated in most German federal states and allows employees to participate in recognized continuing education programs during working hours. Specific requirements and entitlements may vary depending on the federal state.

Legal Entitlement & Duration

Employees are generally entitled to 5 days of educational leave per year, provided they have been employed for at least 6 months. This entitlement cannot be carried over to the following year, except in special cases of accumulation (e.g., 10 days within two years).

Recognition Process

For a seminar to qualify as educational leave,a program must meet the legal requirements of the respective federal states. Our programs are officially recognized as professional continuing education courses and comply with the applicable regulations of the responsible authorities.

Deadlines & Application

The application must usually be submitted to the employer in writing at least 4 to 8 weeks before the start of the seminar. Rejection by the employer is legally only possible if there are compelling operational reasons.

3. Data Collection on This Website

Our internet pages use so-called "cookies". Cookies are small data packages and do not cause any damage to your terminal device. Session cookies are automatically deleted at the end of your visit. Permanent cookies remain stored on your terminal device until you delete them yourself or they are automatically deleted by your web browser.

Cookies can originate from us (first-party cookies) or from third-party companies (so-called third-party cookies). Third-party cookies enable the integration of certain services of third-party companies within websites (e.g. cookies for processing payment services).

Cookies have different functions. Numerous cookies are technically necessary, as certain website functions would not work without them (e.g. the inquiry wizard). Other cookies serve to evaluate user behavior or display advertising. We point out that we use **no third-party tracking cookies**.
